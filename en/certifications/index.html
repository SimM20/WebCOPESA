CERTIFICATES

CERTIFICATES

At COPESA, we are convinced that acting with Corporate Social Responsibility stimulates economic growth, promotes social development, and protects the environment, contributing to sustainable development.

The company takes an active role in generating initiatives and social investment projects to meet community needs, with a strategic and comprehensive perspective, demonstrating respect for ethical values and building common welfare. This is our commitment and challenge for the future.

Due to the pressing need to standardize activities and services provided under globally recognized quality, environmental, safety, and occupational health standards, all human and material efforts were directed toward achieving ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 certification under an Integrated Management System.

In this way, a change in the overall culture of the organization was achieved, uniting the efforts of all employees, while fundamentally seeking customer satisfaction. Critical points of our service are analyzed to pursue a continuous improvement process, evaluating activities, identifying hazards and environmental aspects, and preventing workplace accidents and illnesses in the short and long term. Committed to the environment in which we operate, thinking of today and future generations, we position ourselves in the local market as the first company in this sector certified with a Trinorma Integrated Management System with the following scope:

Road transport of hazardous waste (categories Y8, Y9, and Y11) and non-hazardous waste (MSW – municipal solid waste) using containers. Secondary oil recovery using the swabbing method. Reduction of petroleum waste through incineration in a pyrolytic furnace. Solid cargo lifting services using cranes. Rental of road equipment, cranes, trucks, and vans. Execution of civil works. Provision of personnel for maintenance and environmental remediation on client sites. Road transport services for equipment and supplies for the oil and gas industry. Transport services for hydrocarbons and production water from wells in the San Jorge Gulf basin.

Additionally, we have achieved level 4 safety awarded by the ART, according to Resolution 222 of the Superintendence of Occupational Risks. This level places us among companies that implement safety tools beyond legal requirements, motivating us to continue working under continuous improvement guidelines for the benefit of all parties involved.

INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT SYSTEM POLICY

COPESA is a company in the San Jorge Golfo basin dedicated to road transport of hazardous (Y8, Y9E, and Y11) and non-hazardous (MSW – municipal solid waste) waste using containers. Secondary oil recovery via swabbing, reduction of petroleum waste through pyrolytic incineration, solid cargo lifting using cranes, rental of road equipment, cranes, trucks, and vans for various clients. Civil works execution. Provision of personnel for maintenance and environmental remediation on client sites. Road transport of equipment and supplies for the oil and gas industry. Transport of hydrocarbons and production water from wells in the San Jorge Gulf basin. Tire volume reduction. Tank bottom cleaning. The company operates an integrated management system based on ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 standards, with the following commitments:

  • Meet the requirements specified by our clients.
  • Prevent environmental pollution and protect the environment.
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements in our operations.
  • Establish processes to consult workers or their representatives to reduce risks and eliminate hazards in our operations.
  • Use this document to establish our organization’s objectives and goals. This policy statement allows continuous improvement of the management system; it is publicly accessible, available upon request, and mandatory for our employees, contractors, and suppliers as a condition of employment and contracting.
