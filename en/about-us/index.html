Our History

COPESA initially provided transportation and equipment maintenance services for the company CADIP S.A. Starting in 1990, the company incorporated road equipment, which allowed it to offer earthmoving services. At the same time, it began providing oil and elevated tank transportation services in various oilfields.

In 1994, the company began carrying out industrial/civil works, including the construction of hydrocarbon storage tanks, batteries, heaters, structures, and more, as well as laying gas pipelines, oil pipelines, aqueducts, and building constructions.

In the following years, COPESA continued acquiring new equipment, which allowed it to expand its service portfolio to include: transportation of general and hazardous goods, waste treatment plant, solid cargo transport, and liquid cargo transport.

With over 25 years of experience, we have established ourselves throughout the Golfo San Jorge basin. Today, we operate from bases in the provinces of Santa Cruz, Chubut, Neuquén, and Mendoza, working with various companies such as YPF, SINOPEC, TECPETROL, TERMAP, AESA, PUERTOS PATAGÓNICOS, among others.

Today, COPESA has the technical and financial capacity to meet all service and construction demands in the oil, port, mining, construction, and industrial/civil works sectors.

Vision

The organization's vision is to be a customer-oriented company recognized throughout Argentina for its provision and service of solid and liquid cargo transport equipment, earthmoving, waste treatment, classification, and final disposal, staying at the forefront of technological advancements.

Misión

The mission of the organization is to proactively and reliably provide liquid and solid cargo transportation services, earthmoving, waste treatment, classification, and final disposal, consistently meeting the needs of its clients, based on trust and commitment, and generating sustainable value over time for all involved parties.
