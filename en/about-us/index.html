ABOUT US

Our History

COPESA initially provided transportation and equipment maintenance services for CADIP S.A. Starting in 1990, road equipment was incorporated, enabling earthmoving services. Simultaneously, oil and tank transport services were initiated at various fields.

In 1994, the company began undertaking industrial/civil works, including the construction of hydrocarbon storage tanks, battery installations, heaters, structures, etc., as well as pipeline, aqueduct, and building construction projects.

In the following years, COPESA continued adding new equipment, expanding its services portfolio to include general and hazardous cargo transport, waste treatment plants, solid and liquid cargo transport -

for drilling, workover, and pulling equipment, AIB and surface equipment assembly/disassembly, oil recovery using swabbing equipment, well services, environmental remediation, oil & gas operations, among others.

With over 25 years of experience, we have established ourselves across the San Jorge Gulf basin, currently with operational bases in Santa Cruz, Chubut, Neuquén, and Mendoza, working with various operators such as YPF, SINOPEC, TECPETROL, TERMAP, AESA, PUERTOS PATAGONICOS, among others.

Today, COPESA has the technical and financial capacity to meet all service and project requirements, in oil, port, mining, construction, and industrial or civil works.

Vision

Our vision is to be a client-oriented organization recognized in Argentina for providing and servicing solid and liquid cargo transport equipment, earthmoving, waste treatment, classification, and final disposal, staying at the forefront of technological advancements.

Mision

Our mission is to proactively and reliably provide services in liquid and solid cargo transport, earthmoving, waste treatment, classification, and final disposal, consistently meeting client needs, grounded in trust and commitment, generating sustainable value over time for all involved.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

At COPESA, we are convinced that acting with Corporate Social Responsibility stimulates economic growth, promotes social development, and cares for the environment, contributing to sustainable development.
