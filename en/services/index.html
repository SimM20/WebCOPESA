SERVICES

HAZARDOUS WASTE TREATMENT PLANT & INCINERATION

Our company has a hazardous waste treatment plant certified by the Santa Cruz Provincial Environmental Secretariat. We have a multi-chamber incinerator using controlled pyrolysis technology, with certified ash final disposal. Facilities are private and enclosed.

OIL WASTE TRANSPORT

Collection, transportation, and final disposal of solid waste generated by oil industry operations. The company also provides dumpsters for rent in different sizes: 5 m³ - 8 m³.

TANK CLEANING

We have modern equipment for cleaning storage tanks commonly used in the oil industry. Our fleet includes trucks with state-of-the-art hydro washers, “Vactor” super vacuum trucks, conventional vacuum trucks, and more.

CRANES

We provide mobile telescopic crane services from 16 to 70 tons, with boom lengths from 28 to 50 meters. Equipped with joystick controls, onboard computers, stability sensors, and built-in scales. Operational bases in Las Heras, Caleta Olivia, and Comodoro Rivadavia ensure optimal logistics and reduced downtime.

HYDROCRANES - LIFTS

We offer dual lifting services with hydrocrane + reinforced lift systems, operated by remote control. Certified machinery and operators.

AIB ASSEMBLY & DISASSEMBLY

We provide assembly and disassembly of individual pumping units, AIB alignment, and transportation. We have extensive experience, using modern cranes and qualified personnel.

CRANE - HYDROCRANE - LIFT RENTAL

We provide rental equipment for lifting services, including cranes, hydrocranes, and lifts of different capacities tailored to your needs.

SWABBING

Piston-based extraction method for oil wells without mechanical pumping systems or temporarily abandoned wells. We use recently acquired state-of-the-art equipment.

LIQUID CARGO TRANSPORT

Transport and recovery of all types of fluids. High-vacuum units 32m³ with independent suction/discharge pump. Vactor equipment 9m³ + 3m³ semi-automatic with wireless analog joystick and built-in spray lance. Conventional vacuum units 9m³.

SOLID CARGO TRANSPORT

Transport of materials for drilling rigs, rods, tubing, accessories. Transport of hydrocarbon storage tanks. Oilfield trucks for equipment transport.

CIVIL WORKS

We have suitable facilities and specialized personnel for precast production of structural elements, retaining walls, partitions, foundations, anchor blocks, prestressed and post-tensioned elements. We specialize in industrial and civil works, including construction of petroleum/water storage tanks, heaters, separators, collectors, satellites, and structures.

EARTHMOVING

Extensive experience in road construction, maintenance, and grading. Location construction. Our fleet includes bulldozers, watering trucks, backhoes, loaders, motor graders, compactors, dump trucks, and tipper trucks.

PIPE ASSEMBLY

We specialize in pipeline assembly for oil, gas, and water, including satellites, collectors, and connections.
